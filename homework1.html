Toddlers are Tornados

Do you have a toddler?

Two year olds

I love my toddler, even though she is like a tornado. She is the light of my life, who also drives me crazy.

The drawing on the walls, screaming, crying, inability to accept the word no, and their unspoken perfect timing of kisses.

Watching her run down the hall after a long shift at work just to greet me and ask if I am happy.

Two going on twenty

It is amazing how much they pick up from us without us knowing. One day she is my sweet sleeping bundle of joy and another shes bringing me a water and wash cloth because I am sick.

Why is she growing up so fast?

And dont forget about the endless why questions?

  • Why is the sky blue?
  • Are you happy momma?
  • Can I go outside?
  • You love me?

Sometimes, they drive you mad. Other times they bring you joy. This is why I believe toddlers are like tornados.
